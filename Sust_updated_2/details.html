There are five types of volunteers:

    1.Tourist Guide.
    2.Residence Provider.
    3.Food Guide.
    4.Lifeguard.
    5.Carpooling Volunteer.

1. You can become a Tourist Guide and guide tourists through their journeys. You can earn some remunerations doing it.


2. You can become a Residence Provider. Keep tourists at your house as paying guests.


3. If you are a foodie, you can be a Food Guide. You can suggest popular foods in your area and eating places.


4. If a tourist face any trouble and need help you can be a Lifeguard and help the tourist.


5. You act as a Carpooling Volunteer by providing lifts with your own car or motorcycle. The tourists will pay you for the rides.

If You are willing to do any of these as a volunteer!
